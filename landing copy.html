Infrastructure-Backed Execution

Embedded within Grupo INDI, INDI Lab leverages national-scale construction capacity to reduce R&D risk and accelerate implementation through real-world projects.


Prototype–Deploy–Scale Pipeline

We turn concepts into pilots, and pilots into replicable solutions—heavy focus on data driven scaling, and IP retention that can be licensed or scaled through public funding.


Ecosystem Leverage

Additional Projects

INDIx projects are as flexible and dynamic as urban life itself. Here are a few of the additional fronts we’re actively exploring:


Housing.

We’re doing research around alternative models of affordable housing, ownership, and financing to address structural barriers in the housing system and unlock more inclusive pathways to home access.


Organizational Consulting.

We provide tailored consulting services focused on process optimization, digital tools, and internal policy improvements, enhancing how teams work across sectors and domains.


Policy Design and Legislative Strategy

Our work doesn’t stop at the prototype: we help translate successful pilots into actionable policy and support the drafting of legislative frameworks that can scale innovation responsibly.


Urban Sensors and Micro-Scale Data.

We’re testing sensor networks that capture environmental and behavioral small-scale data: air quality, water levels, microclimates, UV index, noise, and pedestrian flows, to inform citywide strategies for public health and urban livability.
