Who We Are
Innovation at the Intersection of Cities, Infrastructure, Technology, and Community

INDIx is Grupo INDI’s urban innovation lab and venture platform, an incubator and R&D engine that mobilizes shared resources such as data, infrastructure, capital, talent, and institutional knowledge to accelerate execution and scale transformative urban solutions.

We design, prototype, and pilot solutions that address the most urgent urban challenges, positioned at the convergence of engineering, technology, and city life.

Scalable solutions that evolve into social impact-generating public infrastructure programs, deployable across cities.
Civic tech and gov tech ventures with the potential to spin off into public-private partnerships or commercial offerings.
Applied research, data analysis, and strategic studies that not only support implementation but also contribute to global urban discourse and policymaking.

By tapping into Grupo INDI’s deep bench of talent and technical expertise, we’re able to respond fluidly to the evolving needs of our clients, partners, and collaborators, whether they require bold experimentation or operational precision.
